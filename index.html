The Felicia Foundation was established in loving memory of Mrs. Felicia Blankson, a devoted mother, educator, and caregiver who dedicated her life to nurturing young children. She served as a nursery teacher, Sunday School teacher, and later a home childminder. Her love for children and passion for education continue to inspire generations.

The Foundation supports Little Angels Academy by strengthening educational opportunities and providing a positive learning environment for children in the Central Region of Ghana.

  • Teacher training and educational resources
  • Teaching and learning materials
  • Free healthy school feeding programme
  • Scholarships for children in need
  • Provision of a 15-seater school bus
  • Permanent school facility in Nkontrodo, Cape Coast
