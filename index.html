About Little Angels Academy

Little Angels Academy is a growing educational institution located at Ankaful-Ramblers, Cape Coast, in the Central Region of Ghana. Founded on October 4, 2022, the school began with an in-house teacher training programme, laying a strong foundation for quality education. We officially welcomed our first students on October 7, 2024.

Currently, Little Angels Academy offers classes from Creche to Basic 1, with plans to expand each academic year. Our curriculum is guided by the Ghana Education Service (GES) framework and thoughtfully enriched with elements of the UK Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), ensuring a well-rounded approach to early childhood development.

Our Vision
To create an inclusive, creative, and nurturing learning environment where every child is empowered to thrive.

Our Mission
To serve as a dynamic, inclusive, faith-centered learning community that fosters positive educational experiences and meaningful opportunities through strong partnerships among parents and guardians, teachers, and learners, while equipping every child with strong academic foundations, moral values, and leadership skills.

The Felicia Foundation was established in loving memory of Mrs. Felicia Blankson, a devoted mother, educator, and caregiver whose passion for children and education continues to inspire generations.

The Foundation supports Little Angels Academy by strengthening educational opportunities and improving learning environments in the Central Region of Ghana.

  • Teacher training and professional development
  • Teaching and learning materials
  • Free healthy school feeding programme
  • Scholarships for children in need
  • Provision of a 15-seater school bus
  • Permanent school facility in Cape Coast
